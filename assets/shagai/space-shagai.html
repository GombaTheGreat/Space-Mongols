The Game

First player to reach Space 16 on the track wins. Players alternate turns — you throw, then your opponent throws.

How to Throw

Click and drag the bone backward from its resting spot. The further you pull, the harder the throw. Release to flick. A trajectory ghost shows your projected path before you let go.

The Faces

🐎 HORSE — +4 spaces
🐪 CAMEL — +2 spaces
🐑 SHEEP — +1 space
🐐 GOAT — no move
⚡ WALL — no move

Streak Bonus

Land HORSE on consecutive turns and earn +1 bonus space from the second horse onward. Any other face resets your streak.

The Field

Every thrown bone stays on the lane permanently. Resting bones have collision — your bone will deflect off them. The field grows more cluttered as the game progresses.

Wind & Thrusters

A wind indicator appears before each throw. The bone drifts in that direction mid-flight. Occasionally the Drift fires its positioning thrusters — a strong wind spike that affects both players equally.

Opponents

Tuya aims short and safe — one pip difficulty. Chiri is unpredictable — two pips. Solongo is precise and unforgiving — three pips.
